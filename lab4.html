Bacon Tomato Grilled Cheese
__________________________

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 2 slices of gouda cheese
  • 2 slices of swiss cheese
    • 3 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of tomatoes

Instructions

Step 1

  • Preheat skillet

Step 2

  • Cook bacon until it reaches your desired level of crunchyness or chewyness. Once cooked and cooled off, cut all your bacon in half (horizontal cut) to better fit inside the sandwich.

Step 3

  • Assemble your sandwhich by layering your ingredients. Start with either type of cheese, half of your bacon, tomatoes, other half of bacon, and finally the second type of cheese (the order in which you selected your cheese does not matter as it will belnd together anyways).

Step 4

  • Butter one side of the bread and place the butter side down on the hot skillet. Cook until golden brown (or lighter or darker deepending on how crispy you like your bread).

Step 5

  • Before flipping the sandwich, butter the top side of the sandwich. Once buttered, flip the sandwich and cook until it reaches you desired level of doneness.

Step 6

  • Cut sandwich in half (while no cutting direction is objectively the best, diagonal is very visually appealing) and enjoy! Be sure to bring napkins!
